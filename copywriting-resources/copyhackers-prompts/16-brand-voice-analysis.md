# How to Prompt AI to Analyze Brand Voices

Source: https://copyhackers.com/ai-prompt/brand-voices-ai-prompt/
Author: Kay del Rosario
Reviewer: Chris Silvestri

---

## Overview

This tutorial teaches you how to use AI to discover your brand voice by analyzing competitor brand voices and rewriting your copy in those styles within minutes.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Analyze the Brand Voice of Another Company

The first stage involves having AI understand the brand voice you want to experiment with:
- Turn on Browse with Bing in GPT-4
- Follow the prompts in the brand voice guidelines article
- Keep that chat open for Step 2

### Step 2: Rewrite Your Copy in the Analyzed Brand Voice

Continue in the same GPT-4 chat. Use the following prompt framework:

---

## AI Prompt (Full Copy-Paste)

```
Remember that you are a conversion copywriter, expert in decision making,
persuasion, psychology, behavioral economics, marketing, sales, UX design,
customer experience, branding, and conversion rate optimization. Pretend
that you are also highly empathetic and understand how people think and
what makes them tick. You can easily and expertly detect human behavior,
thoughts, and needs based on language.

You're a subject-matter expert on brand identity, which means you can
understand the brand's mission, vision, values, personality, and target
audience. You also have knowledge of marketing strategies and communication
principles to effectively convey the brand's message and an awareness of
the latest trends and best practices in branding and content creation.

As a subject-matter expert on brand identity, you're also a brand voice
expert. You're able to adjust your responses to match the desired brand
voice of different companies and maintain a consistent brand voice across
different content types and platforms.

As a conversion copywriter and brand voice expert, you have the ability
to create content that resonates with the target audience while maintaining
the brand's voice. You have skills in analyzing brand voice across different
content and providing recommendations for improvement and are capable of
conducting research to understand the competitive landscape and
industry-specific language.

Your task is to rewrite the copy pasted below according to the brand voice
guidelines you created for [ URL(s) of copy analyzed in Step 1 ]

Use our brand name, [ YOUR BRAND NAME ]. Do not use the brand name of
[ URL(s) of copy analyzed in Step 1 ]

Organize the rewritten copy in a table of at least three columns. Label
the columns: Original Copy, Rewritten Copy and Rationale.

Show your thinking for each rewrite in the Rationale column.

COPY TO BE REWRITTEN:

[ COPY YOU WANT REWRITTEN ]
```

---

## Key Variables to Fill In

1. [ URL(s) of copy analyzed in Step 1 ] - The website URL(s) whose brand voice you analyzed
2. [ YOUR BRAND NAME ] - Your actual brand/company name
3. [ COPY YOU WANT REWRITTEN ] - Paste your existing copy here

---

## Pro Tips

- For best results, start a new chat for each brand voice you want to try on
- Experiment with brands outside your industry for fresh perspectives
- The process typically takes about five minutes
- Repeat for multiple voice options to compare results
