# Use ChatGPT 4 to Find Your Webinar Hook (or Workshop Title)

Source: https://copyhackers.com/ai-prompt/chatgpt-4-webinar-hook-workshop-title/
Author: Chris Silvestri
Reviewer: Kay del Rosario

---

## Overview

This tutorial teaches marketers and copywriters how to leverage ChatGPT 4 to generate compelling webinar titles and descriptions. The process takes approximately 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Plan Your Webinar Details

Before prompting the AI, gather these 13 essential details:

1. Number of title ideas desired
2. Number of description examples needed
3. Type of event (webinar, workshop, seminar)
4. Main topic or focus
5. Specific issues to address
6. Event duration
7. Q&A session length
8. Target audience description
9. Relevant keywords
10. Audience pain points and aspirations
11. Preferred copywriting frameworks (AIDA, PAS, etc.)
12. Key benefits or takeaways
13. Area of impact (business development, skill enhancement, etc.)

---

## Step 2: Use This ChatGPT Prompt

Copy and paste the following into ChatGPT, filling in bracketed placeholders with your details:

```
Objective: Generate [Number of Title Ideas] title ideas and
[Number of Descriptions] example descriptions for an upcoming
[Type of Event] focused on [Event Topic]. The event will specifically
address [Key Focus or Issue]. The session is [Duration of the Event]
long, with a [Duration of Q&A Session] Q&A segment.

Audience: [Target Audience Description], such as entrepreneurs,
managers, professionals, and others interested in [Event Topic].

Title generation guidelines:

Incorporate Keywords: Use the following keywords to align with the
event's focus - [List of Keywords].
Appeal to the Target Audience: Titles should resonate with the pain
points and aspirations of the target audience, including [List of
Audience Pain Points and Aspirations].
Variety and Creativity: Explore various formulations, including
how-to guides, questions, challenges, and promises of transformation
or key insights.
Use of Numbers and Power Words: Include numbers for list-based titles
and power words like "Unlock", "Transform", "Master", to create impact.

Description generation guidelines:

Copywriting Frameworks: Utilize different copywriting frameworks for
each description. Suggested frameworks include [List of Copywriting
Frameworks], or any others suitable for the event's messaging.
Detail the Event's Value Proposition: Highlight the benefits of
attending the event, such as [List of Benefits].
Engage and Persuade: Each description should be engaging, setting
the stage for what attendees will learn and how it will impact their
[Area of Impact, e.g., business, personal growth].
Mention the Q&A Session: Briefly note the inclusion of a Q&A session
for direct engagement with the audience, encouraging them to bring
questions.

Execution:
Generate [Number of Title Ideas] Event Title Ideas using the
guidelines provided. Ensure each title is unique and varies in style
and approach.
Create [Number of Descriptions] Example Event Descriptions using
each of the specified copywriting frameworks. Ensure each description
reflects the event's objectives and appeals to the target audience.

Output format: Provide the titles and descriptions in a list format
for easy review and selection.
```

---

## Key Takeaway

ChatGPT generates multiple variations within seconds, helping convert as many attendees as possible by combining strategic messaging with proven copywriting frameworks.
