Author: Chris Silvestri
Reviewer: Vesna Mirosavljev

## Overview

This tutorial teaches you to use ChatGPT to generate 50 compelling tagline ideas for your brand in approximately 5 minutes. The method involves setting up AI with specific instructions, then feeding it brand information to produce authentic, memorable options.

---

## Step 1: Assign ChatGPT a Copywriter Role

Prompt to copy-paste:

```
You are a conversion copywriter, expert in decision making, persuasion,
psychology, behavioral economics, marketing, sales, UX design, customer
experience, branding, and conversion rate optimization. Pretend that you
are also highly empathetic and understand how people think and what makes
them tick. You can easily and expertly detect human behavior, thoughts,
and needs based on language. You are also highly skilled in using criminal
and intelligence investigation techniques to understand and analyze buying
behavior and decision-making processes.

I'm going to share information about a company so you can write 50 tagline
ideas for me. Wait for the information and for now, just let me know you
got it.
```

ChatGPT will acknowledge it's ready to proceed.

---

## Step 2: Share Brand Information

Prompt to copy-paste:

```
Here's the information, with this, write 50 compelling, memorable and
authentic tagline ideas for a [CATEGORY/PRODUCT] company called
[COMPANY NAME].

[PASTE BRAND'S INFORMATION HERE]
```

Gather approximately 1,800 words from the company's About page, mission statement, values, and brand narrative. The more detailed information provided, the better the results.

---

## Bonus: Generate Taglines Without Brand Information

Use proven tagline formulas when you lack background information:

Prompt to copy-paste:

```
You are a conversion copywriter, expert in decision making, persuasion,
psychology, behavioral economics, marketing, sales, UX design, customer
experience, branding, and conversion rate optimization. Pretend that you
are also highly empathetic and understand how people think and what makes
them tick. You can easily and expertly detect human behavior, thoughts,
and needs based on language. You are also highly skilled in using criminal
and intelligence investigation techniques to understand and analyze buying
behavior and decision-making processes.

Write 50 compelling, memorable and authentic tagline ideas for a
[PRODUCT TYPE] company called [COMPANY NAME] using the following formulas:

[COPY-PASTE FORMULAS FROM THIS ARTICLE]
```

## Example Results

For Exhale Coffee (ecommerce):
- "Breathe Deep, Sip Slowly, Live Fully."
- "Crafted for Health, Brewed for Taste."
- "From Bean to Being: Coffee for Life."
- "Sip Into Your Best Life."

These serve as starting points for team discussion and refinement.

---

## Tips

- Limit word count by adding constraints (e.g., "Use a maximum of 4 words")
- Method works for both ecommerce and SaaS companies
- Results provide kickoff material for collaborative team feedback rather than final selections
