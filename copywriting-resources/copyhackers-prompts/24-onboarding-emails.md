# How to Prompt AI to Write Onboarding Emails for a New Product

Source: https://copyhackers.com/ai-prompt/write-onboarding-emails-with-ai/
Author: Kay del Rosario
Reviewer: Chris Silvestri

---

## Overview

This tutorial teaches you to use GPT-3.5 to create an email onboarding campaign for new products, even when you lack extensive customer data. The process takes approximately 10 minutes and works for SaaS startups, makers, and growth marketers launching to beta audiences.

---

## Step 1: Gather Your Data

Collect all available information about your product:
- Feature details and capabilities
- Customer/user personas
- Usage instructions
- Existing user feedback

Pro Tip: Use The Value Prism method to uncover deeper product insights.

---

## Step 2: Prompt GPT-3.5 to Write Your Campaign

### Base Prompt Template

```
I'm launching a new product with a very small audience and I need you to write
an email onboarding campaign for new customers who've never used this product before.
The name of the new product is [product name].

[Desired instructions]

Use the information below to write the different emails: [data]
```

### Key Specifications to Include

- Campaign length: Number of emails desired
- Campaign purpose: Goals (e.g., encouraging app returns, feature adoption)
- Subject line variations: Request multiple options for A/B testing
- Content structure: One topic per email focusing on specific features
- Visual elements: Instructions to include screenshots, videos, or help docs
- Call-to-action: Requests for reviews or feature feedback
- Engagement prompt: Invite readers to reply with questions
- Tone and voice: Desired emotional response (excitement, confidence, etc.)
- Reading level: Preferred complexity

---

## Step 3: Optimize and Launch

Review the generated campaigns, refine copy for engagement, add visual assets, and deploy the sequence as your product evolves with customer data.
