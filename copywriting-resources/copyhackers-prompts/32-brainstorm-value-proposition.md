## Overview

This guide teaches marketers and newsletter writers how to use ChatGPT 3.5 to generate unique value propositions (USPs) for email newsletter signups, improving conversion rates beyond generic CTAs.

---

## AI Prompt Instructions

### Step 1: Generate USP Ideas

Core Prompt Template:

```
Brainstorm for me at least {desired number} value propositions for my email
newsletter. {Include any other information that you think will help GPT-3.5
give you more personalized suggestions.}
```

Key Information to Include:
- Frequency of newsletter distribution
- Special offers or discounts for subscribers
- Exclusive "insider" content details
- Other subscriber benefits and newsletter value delivery specifics

Example Applied Prompt (UPPERCASE Magazine):
The tutorial demonstrates seeding ChatGPT with copied website content about the newsletter, then requesting "at least 10 value propositions" with context about timing and exclusive benefits.

---

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Prompt GPT-3.5 to Brainstorm
1. Provide newsletter details (send schedule, exclusive perks, content focus)
2. Request minimum quantity of USP suggestions
3. Copy relevant descriptions from your website into the prompt
4. Submit to ChatGPT 3.5

### Step 2: Use Generated USPs in Copy
1. Review ChatGPT's suggestion list
2. Select 1-3 strongest propositions
3. Integrate language directly into newsletter signup forms
4. Test individually or combine multiple suggestions

## Key Takeaway

Seed more data about your newsletter into the chat to get more relevant USP suggestions for testing variations individually or combined.
